Chicken Pepperoni

Our Chicken Pepperoni Pizza is a crowd favorite, bringing together the perfect balance of bold flavors and comfort. Featuring a crisp, hand-tossed crust, this pizza is topped with tender, grilled chicken, savory pepperoni, and a blend of melted mozzarella and provolone cheeses. The combination of juicy chicken and crispy pepperoni creates a mouthwatering contrast, while a rich, tangy tomato sauce ties it all together..

Finished with a sprinkle of Italian seasoning and a dash of fresh basil, every bite is a satisfying fusion of smoky, savory, and slightly spicy notes. Whether you're enjoying a casual night out or sharing with friends, our Chicken Pepperoni Pizza at Pablo is sure to be a hit.

Sesama Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese delicacy made with vinegared rice combined with various ingredients, such as raw fish, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. The rice, known as shari, is often seasoned with sugar and salt, giving it a unique flavor. Sushi can come in various forms, including nigiri (hand-pressed), maki (rolled), and sashimi (sliced raw fish served without rice).

Each bite is a blend of textures and flavors, offering a delightful and aesthetically pleasing culinary experience. 🍣
